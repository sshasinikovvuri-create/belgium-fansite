🍫 Chocolate Mastery

Belgium produces over 220,000 tons of high-grade chocolate every single year. Brussels National Airport is recognized globally as the single largest sales point for chocolate on Earth.

🍟 Origin of Fries

Despite being labeled as "French Fries" globally, this culinary staple originates from Belgium. Traditional Belgian fries are double-fried and uniquely paired with premium mayonnaise.

🏰 Medieval Castles

Belgium features the highest density of castles per square kilometer in the entire world, providing majestic historic footprints from the Middle Ages across every province.

🇪🇺 The Political Capital

As the primary seat of the European Union, Brussels acts as the hosting capital for major international administrative entities, including NATO and the European Commission.

💎 Diamond Capital

Antwerp is the diamond capital of the world. Roughly 84% of all rough diamonds and 50% of all cut diamonds in the world pass through the city's diamond district.

🎨 Comic Book Pioneers

Belgium has more comic strip artists per square kilometer than any other country. It is the birthplace of world-renowned series like *The Adventures of Tintin* and *The Smurfs*.
